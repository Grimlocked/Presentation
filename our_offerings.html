Offerings from our local butcher

we use a local butcher to process our beef for you. These are the most popular cuts that people ask for, and you can pick any mix and match of cuts you would like that can be accomodated by the size of the cow

Prime Rib

The King Of Beef Roasts And An Excellent Choice For Festive Occasions. These Well-Marbled, Well-Aged Roasts Are As Impressive And Delightful As They Are Flavorful And Tender.

T-Bone steaks

Just say the name “T-bone” and you’ll automatically grab the attention of beef fanatics at your next steak dinner. The T-bone steak is the little brother of the larger Porterhouse, but despite its smaller size is every bit as flavorful and delicious. The T-bone is a classic steak and has a small filet mignon on one side and a meaty New York strip on the other.

Ribeye Steaks

Easy to love and hard to screw up, this ribeye's fatty cap and perfectly distributed marbling make it the go-to steak selection for busy (but hungry) home chefs.

Skirt Steaks

skirt steak is rich with marbling for a wonderful beef flavor and tender texture. This cut surprises with deep flavor that rivals much more expensive cuts and is perfect for the grill. Cook over high heat, turn once and cut against the grain for a steak that will amaze at the dinner table. Our skirt steak is also the perfect starting point for mouth-watering and authentic fajitas.

Flat Iron Steak

Cut from the chuck (shoulder) the flat iron is a fantastic combination of rich beef flavor and tender, meaty bite. This steak is amazingly versatile, simply throw it on the grill or prepare with your favorite marinade for an extra kick of flavor.

Filet Mignon

Filet mignon are a top-seller no matter the time of year. Our butcher hand selects the tenderloin and hand cuts and trims each perfect Filet Mignon. This is a special steak with a deliciously mild flavor and amazing buttery texture. possible. These are consistently our top selling steaks.

Tenderloin Roast

These mouth-watering roasts are versatile and amazingly tender with a buttery texture. Where other cuts get their delicious texture from marbling, the tenderloin is naturally tender and low in fat. Whether roasted whole or cut into steaks, our roasts are sure to impress your family and friends.

Brisket

Whether you’re a BBQ pitmaster or the best barbecuer in your neighborhood, you’ll love our briskets.Our beef briskets are great smoked, braised, or barbecued and are perfect for your next large gathering. These work wonders with your favorite dry rubs, mops or marinades and are ideal for slow cooking.

Individually Sealed Burgers

We offer individually wrapped burger patties. These 1/4 lb. patties are perfect if you only need to cook one or twenty you can pull just the amount you need for dinner or entertaining.

Ground Beef

Nothing goes to waste with our animals! whatever meat is not used for other cuts is used in our ground beef. The beef is packaged into individual 1 lb bags for your convienence.
